Founder

Gerry Adams

‏
Gerry Adams is widely recognized as a pioneering figure of environmental activism. His commitment to environmental issues reached its pinnacle with the establishment of POOKIE (Protection of Orangutans Killed in the East). Under Adams' leadership, POOKIE has made significant strides in raising awareness about the threats faced by these remarkable creatures and has implemented innovative conservation initiatives. Adams' tireless efforts in the realm of environmental activism have garnered him widespread recognition, marked by a string of prestigious awards honoring his contributions to the global fight against climate change and biodiversity loss, such as the 2023 Tyler Prize for Enviormental Achievement.
‏


CFO

John McAfee

‏
John McAfee, renowned for his contributions to the world of cybersecurity, has assumed the role of Chief Financial Officer at POOKIE (Protection of Orangutans Killed in the East). Leveraging his financial expertise and entrepreneurial spirit, McAfee brings a unique perspective to the realm of environmental activism. His innovative approach to financial management, honed in the tech industry, is now dedicated to protecting the orangutans and their habitats in the East. Under McAfee's guidance, POOKIE is poised to make significant strides in conservation efforts.

Chief Spokespeople

Jedward

‏
Jedward, the dynamic duo comprised of John and Edward Grimes, have seamlessly transitioned from their pop music stardom to become the charismatic spokespeople for POOKIE (Protection of Orangutans Killed in the East). Renowned for their energetic personalities and commitment to social causes, Jedward's influence extends beyond the music industry. As the faces of POOKIE, they bring a vibrant and youthful energy to environmental advocacy, using their considerable platform to raise awareness about the urgent need for orangutan protection in the East.
